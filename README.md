## üìä Task Breakdown and Work Completed

### **Task 1: Data Exploration and Summary**
- Imported the dataset into Power BI.  
- Analyzed dataset structure, data types, and basic distributions.  
- Calculated summary metrics such as:
  - Average age  
  - Investment participation rates  
  - Common savings objectives  
- Created bar charts, donut charts, and card visuals to summarize key statistics.

---

### **Task 2: Gender-Based Investment Analysis**
- Compared investment preferences across male and female respondents.  
- Analyzed trends across equity, mutual funds, gold, government bonds, and fixed deposits.  
- Built comparative charts to highlight behavioral differences.

---

### **Task 3: Savings Objective Analysis**
- Explored correlations between savings objectives and investment avenues.  
- Examined objectives such as:
  - Capital Appreciation  
  - Growth  
  - Income  
- Developed visuals showing preferred investments for each objective category.

---

### **Task 4: Investment Duration and Monitoring**
- Analyzed how long individuals prefer to invest.  
- Studied monitoring frequency patterns (daily, weekly, monthly, etc.).  
- Created visuals for:
  - Duration distribution  
  - Monitoring habits  
  - Impact on investment choices  

---

### **Task 5: Reasons for Investment**
- Identified the most common motivations for investing, including:
  - Wealth creation  
  - Tax benefits  
  - Fixed or risk-free returns  
- Designed charts to highlight dominant trends.

---

### **Task 6: Source of Information Analysis**
- Explored where investors obtain financial knowledge:
  - Internet  
  - Newspapers  
  - Financial consultants  
  - Television  
- Built visuals to represent reliance on each source.

---

### **Task 7: Final Dashboard Creation**
- Combined insights from all tasks into a multi-page, interactive Power BI dashboard.  
- Implemented:
  - Slicers and filters  
  - Drill-down interactions  
  - Clean, structured layout  
- Delivered a professional, user-friendly analytical dashboard summarizing all findings.
